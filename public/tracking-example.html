Step 2: Configure Your Settings

Update the configuration in client-tracker.js:

  • API_URL: Your API endpoint
  • AUTH_TOKEN: Your authorization token
  • DEFAULT_ORGANIZATION: Your organization ID
  • DEBUG_MODE: Enable/disable console logging
